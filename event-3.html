《在那出現之前 Before Zero》展覽開幕茶會

北藝新媒 111 級畢業展覽《在那出現之前 Before Zero》 誠摯邀請您一同見證展覽正式揭幕 演出藝術家名單｜ 表演一｜Luca Bonaccorsi 來自義大利，導演，數位藝術家， 音樂家。作品探討科技新媒體間的人際關係，以及科技對日常生活狀態的影響。 近年定居臺灣，從事短片與錄像 藝術製作，同時 於各地進行多媒體音樂表演。 Sound and video artist from Italy. His works explore human relationships amid technology and new media and their effects on daily life. In recent years has relocated to Taipei, Taiwan, to pursue his artistic research through multimedia arts practice and live performances. 表演二｜電動橘皮（周喚、林子晴、凌歆慧、陳彥齊|Chou Huan、Lin Tzu-Ching、Ling Xin-Hui、Chen Yen-chi） 《The mission DLC》 既上次周喚、陳彥齊合作的版本一之後,這次與ネクタイ(nekutai)合作推出續集。 以第三人稱雙人遊戲的形式推進敘事,並以Podcast電台主持人的身分帶領整體敘事結 構與節奏。 場景虛實交錯,最終導向一場對演算法控制的荒謬回應。 Following the first version created by Chou Huan and Chen Yenchi, this sequel is a collaboration with Nekutai. The narrative unfolds through a third-person two-player game, guided by podcast hosts who shape the overall storytelling structure and rhythm. Blending virtual and real environments, the work ultimately leads to an absurd response to algorithmic control, portraying a state of looping and escape within sound and image. Long devoted to live electronic music performances, modular synthesizer design, and generative art. Recently exploring the boundary between digital and analog media through a wide range of instruments and devices, deconstructing and reconstructing sound to create distinctive sonic textures. Skilled in computer-free live performance setups, with extensive hands-on experience in Eurorack system building and audio signal flow design.
